What does Luminous mean?

"Luminous" means shining, bright, or giving off light. It can describe something that emits its own light, like the moon or a lamp, or something that appears glowing or radiant.
It can also be used figuratively to describe something clear, intelligent, or inspiring, like a luminous idea or a luminous smile.
